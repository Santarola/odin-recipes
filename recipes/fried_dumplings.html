Fried Dumplings with Cheese and Cream

Fryer Cream Cheese Dumplings are a delicious and innovative twist on traditional dumplings.

Cream cheese air fryer pork dumplings have a delightful and indulgent taste. The combination of savory pork filling and creamy cream cheese creates a flavorful and rich experience.

List of ingredients

  • Dumplings 250g
  • Cheese 100g
  • Cream 100ml
  • Vegetable oil

How to make it

  1. Pour ~ 70ml of oil into the pan
  2. Add dumplings on pour
  3. Wait until dumplings get golden colour on both sides
  4. Fill pour with cream
  5. Add cheese
  6. Wait 3-4 min
  7. Done!
