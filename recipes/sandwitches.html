Buffalo Chicken Sandwitch

This crockpot Buffalo chicken is great for hearty sandwiches that will please those who love Buffalo chicken wings.

This recipe is perfect for those days spent watching football.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 bottle Buffalo wing sauce, divided
  • Half package dry ranch salad dressing mix
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 6 hoagie rolls, split lenghtwise

How to make it

  1. Place chicken breasts into the slow cooker
  2. Cover and cook on low for 6 to 7 hours
  3. Shred chicken in the cooker with two forks. Stir in butter
  4. Pile shredded chicken and sauce onto hoagie rolls. Serve with remaining Biffalo sauce
